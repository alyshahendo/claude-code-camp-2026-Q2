## Notable locations
- **The Temple Square** — has a **fountain**: `drink fountain` to cure thirst (free).
- **Grunting Boar Inn** — east of Temple Square. Use `offer` then `rent` here to SAVE your
  location so quitting doesn't dump you back at the altar.
- **Clerics' Guild** — west of Temple Square.
- **Bakery** (N off west Main St) and **General/grocery store** (N off east Main St) — buy food/water here.
- **Armory** (S off west Main St), **Weapon shop** (N off east Main St) — gear.
- **Guild of Magic Users / Mages** (S off far west Main St), **magic shop** (N there).
- **Guild of Swordsmen / Warriors** (S off far east Main St) — dummy's guild; `practice` here.
- **West Gate** (far west) → Wall Road south, and out of the city west.
- **East end of Main St** → leaves town eastward (toward Newbie Zone / wilderness).
- **By The Temple Altar** → north exit leaves the temple's back toward the countryside (Newbie Zone).

## Shops & inventories
### The Pet Shop  (south off east Main St, one room east of Market Square; exit: n)
Buy with `buy <pet>` or name it with `buy <pet> <name>`. Order with `order <pet> <cmd>`.
| Price (gold) | Pet |
|---|---|
| 1200 | wolf |
| 900 | rottweiler |
| 600 | beagle |
| 300 | puppy |
| 300 | kitten |
_A pet could serve as a combat ally toward the Minotaur goal._

## Mobs seen
- **Peacekeepers / cityguards / janitor** — town NPCs in the squares. Do NOT attack; they
  will kill a level-1 character. (`consider` before ever engaging anything.)
- **Puff** — a friendly dragon that wanders and spouts one-liners; harmless chatter.

## Dangers / notes
- Character starts hungry & thirsty; keep fed/watered or stats suffer.
- The Newbie Zone (goal area with the Massive Minotaur) lies out through the temple's
  back-north exit and/or the east edge of town — explore carefully and `consider` mobs first.
